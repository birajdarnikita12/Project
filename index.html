OUR MOTTO

"The meaning of life is to find your gift.The purpose of life is to give it away."

People always ask me that, how it feels to be an orphan.
I dont't know. I want to ask them how it feels
to have a family.

The Saved DREAMS Foundation in Pune has taken the initiative
to support the Orphans. Swapnali , the founder along with her team,
student interns from different colleges help underprivileged
children to learn , to overcome their problems, to stand on their own feet
and complete their dreams and make them self dependent.

A good deed is doing wonders and bringing change in the lives
of these little souls through education.
Join hands and support the noble cause
and spread happiness

What We Do

We Provide We Educate We Care

We provide children with instruments of necessity such as books, stationery and food as and when required
We provide education to children currently studying in school and also for those seeking admissions.
We help children with special needs and provide special attention to children involved in substance abuse. LUF is also working towards women menstrual hygiene
